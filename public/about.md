# ExFu

**ExFu** (Executive Function) helps busy professionals set up a personal AI assistant in one afternoon.

## What ExFu Is

ExFu is a hands-on AI setup service. In a single video call session, founder Alastair Brayne works with you to build a personalised AI assistant that knows your context, your preferences, how you work, and how to help you get stuff done.

The session is collaborative: you're not just watching someone configure things. You learn what's being set up and why, so you can maintain and improve it independently.

## The Name

- **ExFu** /ˈeks.fuː/ (noun): Executive function. The cognitive processes that let you plan, prioritise, start things, and see them through.
- **ExFu** /ˈeks.fuː/ (noun): Execution fu. Like kung fu, but for getting things done. The craft of turning intent into action, with AI fighting in your corner.
- **ExFu** /ˈeks.fuː/ (adj.): Executive, functioning. The version of you that's on top of it.

## Who It's For

People who:
- Have multiple projects, ideas, and workflows but no perfect tool
- Are using ChatGPT or Claude but haven't crossed from "helpful chats" to "something you'd hand real work to"
- Need a second brain that understands their workflow and actually does stuff for them

Typical profiles: founders, operators, professionals juggling multiple responsibilities, anyone who feels like everything depends on them.

## What Happens in a Session

- **Format**: Video call, shared screen
- **Duration**: One afternoon
- **Approach**: Collaborative build. Alastair explains what's being set up and why, so you understand the setup, not just use it.
- **Outcome**: An AI assistant configured to your context, preferences, and working style. Plus the confidence to keep improving it yourself.

## The Cooking Analogy

A trained chef can improvise from anything in the cupboard because they understand the principles. Most people need recipes. ExFu doesn't send you to chef school. It shows you the essential gadgets, the core recipes, and the basic rules of flavour, so you can start improvising on your own.
